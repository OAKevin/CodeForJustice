Mural Events

Volunteering to help paint murals is an excellent way to express creativity and beautify public spaces. Participants can work with a team to design and paint large-scale murals on building walls or in public areas. This activity is perfect for artists or individuals interested in art and community service.

  • National Mural Day Every May 7th
  • Juneteenth Every June 19
  • Every 6 Months We Choose

Litter Control Events

Volunteering to help pick up trash is a great way to give back to the community and keep the environment clean. Participants can expect to spend a few hours picking up litter along roadsides, parks, or beaches. This activity is perfect for individuals or groups looking to make a positive impact on the environment.

  • International Day of Action for Rivers Every March 13
  • Global Recycling Day Every March 18
  • World Cleanup Day Every Third Saturday of September 9/16/2023

Lawn Service/Landscaping Events

Volunteering to help with landscaping or lawn service is a great way to improve the appearance and maintenance of public spaces. Participants can expect to spend a few hours planting trees, flowers, and shrubs, or maintaining lawns and gardens. This activity is perfect for individuals or groups with a green thumb and a desire to improve their local environment.

  • World Planting Day Every March 21
  • Arbor Day Every April 10
  • Earth Day Every April 22
